About

Hi, I'm E. J. June

I'm a student who has tried a lot of things — and failed at a fair share of them too. Somewhere in that mess of starting over, I realized I didn't have one interest, I had several, and I'd been treating that like a problem instead of a strength.

Coffee & Context is what happens when I stop picking just one lane. It's career books next to personal growth notes next to the show I watched last night — because that's actually how my brain works, and I'm betting it's how a lot of yours does too.

I'm currently working on my first ebook, with resume and planner templates coming after that. This whole thing started because I wanted a space I could actually keep up with, built around things I actually care about — and because connecting with people who get it is, honestly, one of my favorite parts of any of this.

If that sounds like you, stick around.
